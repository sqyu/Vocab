## Ajouter une idée qui peut renforcer la précédente
de même que ** [də mɛm kə] ** conj. (~ qn/~ + phrase) just like, just as, in the same way as [EX] ~ la première entreprise a fait faillite, la seconde n'a pas duré très longtemps || *just as* the first business went bankrupt, the second one didn't last very long *either* %% conj. as well as, as does [EX] ~ son prédecesseur, il a démissionné || he resigned *as did* his predecessor
mais aussi ** [mɛ o.si] ** conj. but also
à propos ** [a pʁo.po] ** prep. by the way %% [EX] ~, je …
à propos de ** [a pʁo.po də] ** prep. about (with respect to), with regard to [EX] ~ votre travail || *about* your work [EX] ~ qui? || *about* who?
ainsi que ** [ɛ̃.si kə] ** conj. as, just as, like [EX] c'est ~ l'on faisait le beurre || that's *the way/how* they used to make butter [EX] est-ce ~ tu parles à ta mère? || is that *the way/how* you speak to your mother? %% conj. as well as [EX] l'Italie ~ quatre autres pays d'Europe participe à ce projet || Italy, *along with* four other European countries, is taking part in this project
ainsi ** [ɛ̃.si] ** adv. in this way, thus [EX] ~, … || *thus*, … [EX] l'enfant apprendra ~ à être indépendant || the child will *thus* learn to be independent

de même ** [də mɛm] ** adv. likewise [EX] vous partez? nous ~ || are you leaving? *so are* we [EX] agir or faire ~ || to do *the same* [EX] il a refusé de venir et sa sœur ~ ||  he refused to come and *so did* his sister [EX] il a ~ refusé de venir || he *also* refused to come
de plus ** [də plys] ** adv. furthermore, moreover, what's more %% adv. more than [EX] j'ai mangé deux pommes ~ qu'elle || I ate two apples *more than* she did [EX] donnez-moi deux pommes ~ || give me two *more* apples
de surcroît ** [də syʁ.kʁwa] ** adv. (rather formal) besides, moreover, furthermore [EX] ~, … || *Moreover*, … [EX] c’est une personne très gentille et très intelligente ~ || she’s very kind, and *what’s* more she’s smart.
effectivement ** [e.fɛk.tiv.mɑ̃] ** adv. indeed [EX] le problème est ~ complexe || the problem is *indeed* complex [EX] la police est ~ intervenue trop tard || the police did *indeed* intervene too late %% adv. (rare) really, actually

en effet ** [ɑ̃.n‿e.fɛ] ** adv. indeed [EX] soyez prudent, les routes sont ~ très glissantes || do be careful because the roads are very slippery *indeed* [EX] ‘tu n'étais pas chez toi hier soir?’ —‘~’ || ‘you weren't home yesterday evening?’ —‘no, I wasn't’

(colloquial) moreover, what is more, on top of that [EX] ~ (de cela) || *on top of* that [EX] il est arrivé en retard et ~ (de cela) il a commencé à se plaindre || he arrived late and *what's more/on top of* that he started complaining [EX] les taxes ~ || *plus* tax, tax not included
